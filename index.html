About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher at IISER Bhopal working at the intersection of theoretical physics, nonlinear dynamics, and pattern formation. I’m currently studying Turing systems, chaos-inspired geometric models, and spectral line identification in JWST MIRI datasets as part of NASA's Citizen Scientist program.

Research

Projects

  • Turing & Multi-Morphogen Dynamics: Investigating combinatorial emergence and geometric constraints in reaction-diffusion systems.
  • Unified Dynamic Equation Framework: Developing a mensuration-based approach to encode families of curves through a single formulation.
  • JWST MIRI Spectral Line Identification: Automating region selection and spectral extraction using Python-based workflows.

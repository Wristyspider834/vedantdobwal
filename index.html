about

I am Vedant (joined IISER 2024). My work mixes theory, computation and observational data: from studying pattern-formation and Turing instabilities to developing pipelines for JWST MIRI spectral extraction. I prefer short, reproducible code and clear exposition.

research — active

Turing & multi-morphogen dynamics

Multi-morphogen reaction–diffusion: exploring combinatorial instabilities and richer pattern vocabularies beyond classical two-species models.

Unified dynamic-equation framework

Developing a geometric/mensurational formulation that encodes families of plane curves and dynamic equations sharing invariants (area, integral constraints).

JWST MIRI spectral extraction

Automating region selection and spectral extraction for MIRI cubes using Python (astropy / numpy / scipy) — reproducible pipelines for line identification.

Temporal energetic behavior (theory)

Early-stage theoretical work formalizing temporal energetic measures across dynamical systems and proposing testable constructs.
